PLACE YOUR KRIFY LOGO HERE
===========================

SUPPORTED FORMATS
✓ JPG  (.jpg)
✓ JPEG (.jpeg)
✓ PNG  (.png)
✓ BMP  (.bmp)

Instructions:
1. Copy your Krify logo image (the gear with green center)
2. Paste it in this folder
3. Rename to: krify_logo.[format]
   Examples:
   - krify_logo.jpg
   - krify_logo.png
   - krify_logo.jpeg
   - krify_logo.bmp

4. Restart the app (run launch_groq.bat)
5. Logo will appear in all generated resumes!

Recommended:
- Format: PNG (best quality) or JPG (smaller size)
- Size: 200x200 to 400x400 pixels
- Background: Transparent (PNG) or white (JPG)

The logo will appear centered at the top of every resume.
